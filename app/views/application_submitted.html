What happens next

Your details have been submitted and an operator will call the person you nominated at least once during your chosen time, or as close to the time as possible. (Wednesday, 15 January 2020 at 15:00). When a CLA operator calls, the call will come from an anonymous number.

  • The first person you speak to will be an operator who will ask about your problem and your financial circumstances.
  • If it looks like you might qualify for legal aid you'll be put through to a specialist adviser, who will make the final decision on your case.
  • You will need to provide evidence of your financial circumstances and maybe your problem. Your specialist adviser will explain what is needed.
  • If your specialist adviser thinks you need to speak to someone in person or have someone represent you in court, they will organise this.

If CLA can't help you, we'll always suggest where else you might get help.

If we can do anything to make it easier for you to communicate with us, please tell the operator.

Protecting your personal data and your privacy statement is important to us. Read the full Civil Legal Advice Privacy Statement.

Your session will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity. We do this for your security.
