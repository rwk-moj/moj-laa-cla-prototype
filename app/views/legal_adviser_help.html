A legal adviser may be able to help you

You will usually only get legal aid for advice about clinical negligence if your child has suffered a brain injury during pregnancy, birth or in the first 8 weeks of life.

You should contact a legal aid adviser in your area, who may be able to help.

Your adviser will check whether you qualify for legal aid at no cost to you by asking about your problem and your finances. In some cases you may need to pay a contribution towards your legal aid.
